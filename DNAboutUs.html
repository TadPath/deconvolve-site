About This Site

The Purpose of Deconvolve.Net
Deconvolve.Net exists to showcase and freely distribute my software packages for deconvolution and image processing. The website is a specialist deconvolution-themed spin-off from my general image processing website (www.Bialith.com) and the software I distribute here is the same as that distributed at Bialith. Having worked hard over many years developing these powerful software tools for my own research use, I wanted to share my results and software with others who could make use of them.
Most powerful deconvolution tools (especially on the MS Windows platform) are commercialised closed source products which can only be obtained by those with sufficient funds. However, I believe that these tools should be freely available to everyone - including students, amateurs and hobbyists - so they can learn, develop ideas and enjoy the benefits of deconvolution. I also believe that, at the time of writing, the deconvolution software offered here, while free and open source, is probably some of the most powerful and versatile deconvolution software available - even when compared to the most expenisve commercial products on the market.

Ownership and Funding of Deconvolve.Net
